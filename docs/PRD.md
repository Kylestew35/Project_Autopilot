Product Requirements Document (PRD)

Project Name: Careful Cravings

Executive Summary
Careful Cravings is an AI-powered healthy food recommendation engine that aims to help users make informed and nutritious meal choices. The project's primary goals are to develop an AI-powered recommendation system, increase user engagement and retention, and promote healthier eating habits to reduce the risk of chronic diseases.

Product Overview
Careful Cravings will be a web and mobile application that leverages AI technology to provide personalized healthy food recommendations based on user preferences, dietary needs, and health goals. The application will offer a comprehensive suite of features, including:
- Intelligent meal recommendations
- Personalized meal planning and tracking
- Nutritional information and dietary analysis
- Integration with fitness and health tracking apps

Key Product Features
1. AI-Powered Recommendation Engine
   - Develop an advanced AI model to analyze user preferences, dietary requirements, and health goals
   - Provide personalized meal recommendations based on the user's profile and preferences
   - Continuously refine the recommendation engine through machine learning and user feedback

2. Personalized Meal Planning and Tracking
   - Allow users to create custom meal plans and track their daily food intake
   - Provide detailed nutritional information and dietary analysis for each recommended meal
   - Enable integration with fitness and health tracking apps to holistically monitor user progress

3. User Engagement and Retention
   - Implement a user-friendly and intuitive interface to enhance the overall user experience
   - Develop a comprehensive content strategy to engage users with educational resources and recipes
   - Leverage push notifications and in-app reminders to encourage consistent usage

4. Healthy Eating Promotion
   - Provide educational content and resources to help users understand the importance of a balanced diet
   - Offer meal planning and preparation tips to support users in adopting healthier eating habits
   - Collaborate with nutritionists and healthcare professionals to ensure the accuracy and reliability of the product's recommendations

Product Objectives
1. Achieve a user acquisition target of 50,000 registered users within the first 6 months of launch.
2. Maintain a user retention rate of at least 75% over the first 12 months of operation.
3. Increase the average user engagement time by 30% within the first 6 months of launch.
4. Achieve a customer satisfaction score of at least 4.5 out of 5 within the first 12 months of operation.

Key Risks and Mitigation Strategies
1. Regulatory Compliance
   - Impact: High
   - Mitigation: Closely monitor and adhere to relevant food and health regulations.

2. Data Privacy and Security
   - Impact: High
   - Mitigation: Implement robust data privacy and security measures to protect user information.

3. AI Model Accuracy
   - Impact: Medium
   - Mitigation: Continuously refine and optimize the AI recommendation engine to improve accuracy.

4. User Adoption
   - Impact: Medium
   - Mitigation: Develop a comprehensive marketing and user engagement strategy to drive adoption.
