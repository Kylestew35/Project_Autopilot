Product Requirements Document (PRD)

Project Name: Fish Lake Family Reunion

Executive Summary
The Fish Lake Family Reunion project aims to bring the entire family together for a memorable and enjoyable reunion experience. The project will involve planning and executing a range of activities, accommodations, meals, and travel coordination to cater to all age groups and ensure a smooth and stress-free process.

Project Objectives
1. Secure suitable accommodations for all attendees at Fish Lake.
2. Curate a diverse range of activities and entertainment options for all age groups.
3. Provide catering services that cater to the dietary needs and preferences of all attendees.
4. Coordinate the transportation and travel logistics for all attendees.
5. Maintain clear and effective communication with all family members throughout the planning and execution process.
6. Ensure a smooth and enjoyable family reunion experience for all attendees.
7. Gather feedback and identify areas for improvement for future family reunions.

Project Phases and Workstreams
1. Planning Phase
   1. Venue and Accommodations: Research and book suitable cabins and campsites at Fish Lake.
   2. Activities and Entertainment: Curate a diverse range of activities and entertainment options for the reunion.
   3. Catering and Meals: Plan and coordinate the meals, snacks, and refreshments for the event.
   4. Transportation and Travel: Organize transportation logistics and provide travel guidance for attendees.
   5. Communication and Coordination: Establish communication channels and coordinate with family members.

2. Execution Phase
   1. Venue and Accommodations Setup: Ensure the cabins, campsites, and common areas are prepared for the event.
   2. Activity and Entertainment Coordination: Facilitate the planned activities and provide support for family members.
   3. Catering and Meal Service: Oversee the meal preparation, serving, and cleanup during the event.
   4. Transportation and Travel Support: Assist attendees with any transportation or travel-related needs.
   5. Communication and Engagement: Maintain open communication and actively engage with family members.

3. Evaluation Phase
   1. Feedback Collection: Gather feedback from attendees to understand their experiences.
   2. Lessons Learned: Analyze the feedback and identify opportunities for improvement.
   3. Documentation and Reporting: Document the overall event and provide a comprehensive report.

Project Risks and Mitigation Strategies
1. Weather Disruptions: Monitor weather forecasts and have contingency plans in place.
2. Accommodation Availability: Book accommodations well in advance and have backup options.
3. Dietary Restrictions: Collect dietary information from attendees and work with caterers.
4. Family Conflicts: Establish clear communication channels and conflict resolution strategies.
5. Budget Overruns: Develop a detailed budget, monitor expenses, and explore cost-saving measures.

Automation and Integration
1. Accommodation Booking Confirmation: Send confirmation email to attendee and update project dashboard.
2. Dietary Preference Notification: Notify catering team and update meal planning.
3. Activity Schedule Update: Update the project calendar and notify all attendees.
4. Travel Coordination Reminder: Send travel guidance and coordination reminders to attendees.
5. Feedback Collection: Initiate feedback survey and compile results for evaluation.
