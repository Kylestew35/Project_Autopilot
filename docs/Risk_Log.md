Risk Log

Risk Name: Resistance to Change
Impact: High
Mitigation:
- Develop a comprehensive change management strategy to address employee concerns and ensure effective adoption of the new system.
- Implement a robust communication plan to keep all stakeholders informed and engaged throughout the project.
- Provide thorough training and support to help users understand the benefits of the new system and build their confidence in using it.

Risk Name: Data Integration Challenges
Impact: Medium
Mitigation:
- Carefully map out the data integration requirements and plan the necessary processes to ensure seamless data flow between the new system and existing systems.
- Allocate sufficient time and resources for thorough testing of the data integration functionality to identify and resolve any issues.
- Establish clear data governance policies and procedures to maintain data integrity and reliability.

Risk Name: Budget Constraints
Impact: Medium
Mitigation:
- Develop a detailed project budget and continuously monitor expenditures to identify and address any cost overruns.
- Explore cost-saving opportunities, such as leveraging existing infrastructure or negotiating favorable vendor contracts.
- Prioritize project activities and allocate resources accordingly to ensure the most critical components are delivered within the available budget.

Risk Name: Vendor Reliability
Impact: High
Mitigation:
- Conduct a comprehensive vendor evaluation and selection process to identify a reliable partner with a proven track record of successful system implementations.
- Establish clear contractual agreements with the vendor, including service-level agreements, performance metrics, and contingency plans.
- Maintain regular communication and oversight of the vendor's progress to ensure timely delivery and quality of the system.
