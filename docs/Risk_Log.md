Risk Log

Project Name: Beef Production Initiative

Risk 1: Supply Chain Disruptions
Impact: Significant impact on production and delivery
Mitigation: Develop a robust supply chain risk management plan and maintain strong supplier relationships. This includes identifying alternative suppliers, diversifying the supply base, and implementing contingency plans to address potential disruptions.

Risk 2: Regulatory Changes
Impact: Potential compliance issues and increased costs
Mitigation: Stay informed on regulatory changes, both at the local and national level, that may impact the beef production process. Proactively adapt processes and procedures to ensure compliance and minimize the impact on operations.

Risk 3: Resistance to Change
Impact: Slow adoption of new processes and technologies
Mitigation: Implement a comprehensive change management strategy to address resistance to change. This includes providing training and support to stakeholders, communicating the benefits of the changes, and involving them in the decision-making process.
