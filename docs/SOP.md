Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)
Auto Pilot AI Project

1. Purpose
The purpose of this SOP is to provide a structured and actionable guide for the successful execution of the Auto Pilot AI project. This project aims to develop an AI-powered project management system that can automate key tasks and decision-making, reducing the time and effort required for project managers to oversee complex projects, and improving project outcomes.

2. Scope
This SOP covers the following phases and workstreams of the Auto Pilot AI project:
- Discovery and Requirements Gathering
  - User Research
  - Requirements Analysis
- System Design and Architecture
  - AI and Machine Learning
  - Integration and Connectivity
  - User Experience
- Development and Testing
  - Software Development
  - Quality Assurance
- Deployment and Adoption
  - Deployment and Infrastructure
  - Change Management
  - Training and Support

3. Roles and Responsibilities
- Project Manager: Oversee the overall project execution, coordinate cross-functional activities, and ensure timely delivery of project milestones.
- Product Manager: Define the product vision, prioritize features, and manage the product roadmap.
- Technical Lead: Architect the system design, lead the software development team, and ensure the technical quality of the solution.
- User Experience Designer: Design the user interface and interaction flows to provide a seamless and intuitive experience for project managers.
- Change Management Lead: Develop and execute the change management plan to facilitate the adoption of the Auto Pilot AI system.
- Training and Support Specialist: Provide comprehensive training and ongoing support to project managers using the Auto Pilot AI system.

4. Key Risks and Mitigation Strategies
- Resistance to Adoption: Implement a robust change management strategy and provide extensive training and support to project managers.
- Data Integration Challenges: Carefully design the integration architecture and establish clear data governance policies.
- AI Model Accuracy and Reliability: Invest in rigorous testing and validation of the AI models to ensure their reliability and accuracy.

5. Automation Workflows
- Project Task Automation: Automatically assign resources, set deadlines, and update project schedules in Jira when new tasks are created.
- Risk Identification and Mitigation: Analyze project data and proactively identify potential risks, then suggest mitigation strategies in Notion.
- Team Communication Coordination: Automatically notify team members and schedule a status meeting in Slack when a new project milestone is reached.

6. Project Timeline
- Discovery and Requirements Gathering: April 1, 2023 - June 30, 2023
- System Design and Architecture: July 1, 2023 - September 30, 2023
- Software Development: October 1, 2023 - March 31, 2024
- Testing and Quality Assurance: January 1, 2024 - April 30, 2024
- Deployment and Adoption: May 1, 2024 - August 31, 2024

7. Project Objectives
- Develop an AI-powered project management system that can automate at least 90% of the tasks typically performed by project managers.
- Reduce the time and effort required for project managers to oversee complex projects by at least 50%.
- Improve project outcomes by leveraging AI to identify risks, optimize resource allocation, and streamline workflows, resulting in a 20% increase in project success rate.
