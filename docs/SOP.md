Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)
Project: Careful Cravings - AI-powered Healthy Food Recommendation Engine

1. Purpose
This SOP outlines the processes and guidelines for the successful execution of the Careful Cravings project, an AI-powered healthy food recommendation engine. It covers the key phases, workstreams, and activities to be undertaken to achieve the project's goals.

2. Scope
The SOP applies to all team members involved in the Careful Cravings project, including but not limited to project management, product design, software development, AI model development, testing, marketing, and customer support.

3. Phases and Workstreams
3.1. Discovery Phase
   3.1.1. Market Research
   3.1.2. User Research
   3.1.3. Competitive Analysis

3.2. Design Phase
   3.2.1. Product Design
   3.2.2. Technical Design
   3.2.3. AI Model Development

3.3. Development Phase
   3.3.1. Frontend Development
   3.3.2. Backend Development
   3.3.3. AI Integration

3.4. Testing Phase
   3.4.1. Functional Testing
   3.4.2. Performance Testing
   3.4.3. User Acceptance Testing

3.5. Launch Phase
   3.5.1. Product Deployment
   3.5.2. Marketing and Promotion
   3.5.3. Customer Onboarding

3.6. Optimization Phase
   3.6.1. User Feedback Analysis
   3.6.2. Performance Monitoring
   3.6.3. Product Enhancements

4. Risks and Mitigation Strategies
4.1. Regulatory Compliance: Closely monitor and adhere to relevant food and health regulations.
4.2. Data Privacy and Security: Implement robust data privacy and security measures to protect user information.
4.3. AI Model Accuracy: Continuously refine and optimize the AI recommendation engine to improve accuracy.
4.4. User Adoption: Develop a comprehensive marketing and user engagement strategy to drive adoption.

5. Automations
5.1. Jira Issue Tracking: Notify project team and update project dashboard for new issues.
5.2. GitHub Commit Monitoring: Trigger automated build and deployment pipeline for new commits.
5.3. Slack User Feedback: Create Jira issue and notify product team for user feedback submissions.
5.4. Notion Task Synchronization: Update project management dashboard for task status updates.

6. Timeline
Refer to the provided project timeline for the scheduled phases and milestones.

7. Objectives
7.1. Achieve a user acquisition target of 50,000 registered users within the first 6 months of launch.
7.2. Maintain a user retention rate of at least 75% over the first 12 months of operation.
7.3. Increase the average user engagement time by 30% within the first 6 months of launch.
7.4. Achieve a customer satisfaction score of at least 4.5 out of 5 within the first 12 months of operation.

8. Roles and Responsibilities
Refer to the project team structure and individual roles for the specific responsibilities of each team member.

9. Reporting and Communication
Regular project status updates, team meetings, and communication channels will be established to ensure effective collaboration and information sharing.

10. Change Management
Any changes to the project scope, timeline, or resources must be reviewed and approved by the project manager and the steering committee.
